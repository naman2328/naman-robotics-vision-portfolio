and how I build differently

01

Real Constraints First
Glare, vibration, inconsistent positioning, timing pressure. I design for factory floors, not controlled labs. Every pipeline accounts for failure modes before they appear.
02

Simulation is a starting point, not a destination. All projects deployed on physical hardware — Dobot arms, Jetson Orin Nano, custom robots. I know the gap between theory and actuation.
